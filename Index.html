Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about Vertex Security

How do I set up Vertex Security?

After inviting the bot, simply run /setup moderate to configure basic protection. The bot will create the necessary channels and roles automatically.

What are the different security levels?

Vertex offers three levels: Relaxed (minimal filtering), Moderate (balanced protection), and Strict (maximum security). You can switch between them anytime with the /security-level command.

Does it work with non-English servers?

Yes! Vertex Security's content filter supports over 50 languages and can detect toxic content, slurs, and spam in multiple languages simultaneously.

Is Vertex Security really free?

Absolutely! Every single feature is completely free with no premium plans, hidden costs, or limitations. It's open-source and will always remain free.

How does the anti-raid system work?

When 5+ users join within 30 seconds, Vertex automatically enables lockdown mode, requiring new members to complete a CAPTCHA verification. Existing members can still chat normally.

Can banned users appeal their punishment?

Yes! Banned users can DM the bot with !appeal and fill out a form. Appeals are sent directly to your moderation team for review.

What are private owner logs?

A separate log channel visible only to server owners that records all moderation actions, providing complete transparency about what happens in your server.

Where can I get support?

Join our Discord support server for live help, check the documentation, or open an issue on our GitHub repository. Our community is always happy to help!
